Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate are the AI-generated questions?
Our AI has been trained on thousands of high-quality MCQs across various subjects and difficulty levels. The accuracy rate exceeds 95% for most topics, and you can always review and edit any questions before using them.
Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan later?
Yes, you can change your plan at any time. When upgrading, we'll prorate the difference. When downgrading, the new rate will apply at the start of your next billing cycle.
What subjects are supported?
MCQGenerator supports a wide range of subjects including sciences, mathematics, humanities, languages, professional certifications, and more. Our AI can generate questions for virtually any subject area with properly structured information.
Is there a free trial available?
Yes, we offer a 7-day free trial with access to our Professional plan features. No credit card is required to start your trial, and you can generate up to 50 questions to test our platform.
